## Where to find it

1. Open **My Team** from the left sidebar.
2. Select a team member.
3. Open the **One-on-Ones** tab.
4. Click **New 1:1**.

## What you can do in a 1:1

- Set the meeting **date**.
- Track the team member **mood**.
- Build a checkbox-based **Agenda**.
- Review **Feedbacks To Share** and mark items as shared.
- Manage **Action Items** with assignee and priority.
- Open **Recent Pull Requests** for the member with filters and grouping.
- Write **Meeting Notes** in markdown.
- Expand **Objectives Checkin** to review current-period objectives.

## Saving behavior

- Changes are autosaved as you edit.
- If you leave with unsaved changes, EM Kit tries to save before navigation.

## Best practices

- Add agenda items before the meeting and check them live.
- Convert outcomes into Action Items before ending the session.
- Use Feedbacks To Share to make feedback delivery consistent.
