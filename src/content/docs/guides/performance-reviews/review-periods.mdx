---
title: Review Periods
description: Set up and manage review cycles for your workspace.
---

## Where to find it

1. Open **Performance** from the left sidebar.
2. In **Review Periods**, use the selector to switch periods.

## Create a review period

1. Click **New Review Period**.
2. Fill in:
- **Title** (for example, `H1 2026`)
- **Date Range**
3. Click **Create**.

## Edit or delete a period

- Click **Edit Period** to update title or dates.
- Click **Delete Period** to remove the period and associated member review data.

## Team Summary table

For the selected period, EM Kit shows:

- Team members
- Review status
- Rating

Click a row to open the member review when available.
